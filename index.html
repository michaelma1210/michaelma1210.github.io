About Me

From Sacramento to Berkeley.

Born and raised in Sacramento, CA, I am currently a senior studying Computer Science at UC Berkeley. I am joining AppDynamics in San Francisco after I graduate in Spring 2018. My interests include backend development, data analytics, and artificial intelligence.

Outside of school, I am active in Klesis Christian fellowship. I like to make short films and recently filmed, directed, and edited two student narrative shorts. I also take some photos on the side, usually as the designated OP for any group trips I'm on.
